The Oral Ecolife™ bacterial panel kit is designed for the detection of Oral microbiota. The test is performed based on quantitative real time PCR (qRT-PCR) method based on Taqman PCR technology. The results are reported based on a widely published and accepted research method of relative quantification. The results are reported as the relative abundance of the bacterial gene to the conserved bacterial gene from the given sample in a certain amount of bacterial DNA.

Test Information: The Oral Ecolife Kit™ measures the composition of microbiota species and host immune markers using modern and culture-in- dependent technologies. Results of this test cannot be used for diagnosis of disease or health conditions. Detection or lack of detection of microorganism or immune marker in this test, does not imply diagnosis of disease or clinical condition. The Oral Ecolife™ test should not replace routine examinations with doctors and healthcare professionals.
