The results show representative numbers proportional to normalised qRT-PCR value.

Test Information: The Female EcologiX™ profile measures the composition of vaginal microbiota species and host immune markers using modern and culture-independent technologies. Results of this test cannot be used for diagnosis of disease or health conditions. Detection or lack of detection of microorganism or immune markers in this test, does not imply diagnosis of disease or clinical condition. The Female EcologiX™ test should not replace routine examinations with doctors and healthcare professionals i.e. smear tests.
