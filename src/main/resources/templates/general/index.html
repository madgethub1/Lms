The Vaginal Ecolife™ bacterial panel kit is designed for the detection of vaginal microbiota. The test is performed based on quantitative real time PCR (qRT-PCR) method based on Taqman PCR technology. The results are reported based on a widely published and accepted research method of relative quantification. The results are reported as the relative abundance of the bacterial gene to the conserved bacterial gene from the given sample in a certain amount of bacterial DNA.

Several types of lactobacilli dominate the healthy vagina; these bacteria produce lactic acid and various antimicrobial compounds. Level of acidic environment of vagina directly proportional to the abundance of vaginal lactobacilli, acidic environment help prevent growth of pathogenic bacteria. Bacterial vaginosis and associated health conditions are mainly characterised by the decrease in the total vaginal abundance of Lactobacilli and corresponding significant increase in the level of anaerobic microbes. The type and level of vaginal lactobacilli varies in different individuals and it depends on various factors like age, ethnicity, life style, medications, infection, injury etc.

Test Information: The Female Ecolife™ profile measures the composition of vaginal microbiota species and host immune markers using modern and culture-independent technologies. Results of this test cannot be used for diagnosis of disease or health conditions. Detection or lack of detection of microorganism or immune markers in this test, does not imply diagnosis of disease or clinical condition. The Female Ecolife™ test should not replace routine examinations with doctors and healthcare professionals i.e. smear tests.
